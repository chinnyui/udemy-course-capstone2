I am a 24 year old digital marketer from south London. I have been working in digital marketing for the last 7 months. Before that I worked as a language assistant for 9 months in Hamburg, Germany.

I did a degree in modern languages between 2017 and 2021. I studied German and Russian languages and cultures, and on my year abroad I went to Jena, Germany for 4 months and Moscow, Russia for 6 weeks (I went in 2020, so after 6 weeks the pandemic hit and I had to fly back to the UK).

My Hobbies


Music

Since the age of 8 I have been involved with music in some way or another, and it is one of my favourite forms of entertainment. At the moment I am a between choirs, but I have spent most of the last decade and a half in a choir of some form, whether it be a church choir, school choir or university choir.

I have also taken part in music through learning musical instruments. During my schooling I took up both the violin and viola, and progressed up to grade 6 in the violin. I have a violin that I still occasionally practice with now, as well as a keyboard (but sadly no viola currently).

Theatre

Since returning to the UK in 2022, and especially in the last 6 months, I have been taking more of an interest in theatre productions. My first play was in late 2022, and it was a production of Shakespeare's As You Like It. Shakespeare is much more entertaining in theatres than how we studying it, and this was the performance that allowed me to realise it.

The most recent production that I saw was Under The Kunde Tree at Southwark Playhouse. This play was an insight into the secret cameroonian independence war against the French in the 1950s. This type of play especially delights me, as it is a very interesting format to be introduced to and learn more about different parts of history, and I was fascinated by a struggle of independence that I never knew even existed.
